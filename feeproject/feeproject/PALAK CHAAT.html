PALAK CHAAT



INGREDIENTS

  • 10 leaves spinach
  • 1/2 cup gram flour
  • 1/2 tbps salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tbps chopped ajwain ke patte
  • 5 teaspoon yogurt(curd)
  • 1 teaspoon sweet tamarind sauce
  • 1 teaspoon green chutney
  • 2 teaspoon vermivelli

INSTRUCTIONS


  1. Firstly, take a fresh spinach leaf and make a batter of gram flour and water. Add salt and pepper to the batter depending upon your taste.
  2. After adding salt and pepper, add 2 tbps of chopped ajwain ke patte to the mixture. Fry it in fresh oil and wait till it crisps and then douse the flame.
  3. Add sweet yogurt, tamarind sauce and green mint chutney to the leaf and garnish the spinach leaf with vermicelli.
  4. Palak chaat is ready to serve.
