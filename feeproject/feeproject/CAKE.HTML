CAKE



INGRIDIENTS

  • 250 GM+1tbps all purpose flour
  • 60 ml butter
  • 125 ml powdered sugar
  • 125 ml curd+2 tbps condensed milk
  • 2 tbps milk powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 50 gm tutti frutti and dry fruit
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 180 ml milk
  • 11 tsp vinegar

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Take a bowl.Add tutti frutti and dry fruits and coat them in 1 tbps flour
  2. Take a big bowl.Add butter and powdered sugar.Mix them till creamy.Add curd,condensed milk and mix it.
  3. Take a sieve.Add all dry ingridients.Sieve them and add to wet ingidients.
  4. Add tutti frutti,dry fruits and vanilla essence.Add milk in small quantities to make ribbon like consistencies.
  5. Add vinegar and pour it in baking tray.
  6. Pre heat the oven at 180 degree and bake the cake for 30-35 min. Cake is ready to serve.
