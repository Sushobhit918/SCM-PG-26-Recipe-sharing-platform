Golgappa



INGRIDIENTS

  • Semolina + salt
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Baking soda
  • Boiled potatao
  • Curd
  • Tamarind sauce
  • Crispy namkeen
  • Lemon

INSTRUCTIONS


  1. In a medium-sized,add 1 cup fine semolina,2 tablespoon flour,1/8 teaspoon Baking soda and pinch of salt. Mix well.
  2. Now add water,1-2 tablespoons at a time, and knead a smooth elastic dough that is neither too soft nor too stiff
  3. It will take about 8-10 min to knead the dough
  4. Cover the dough with a damp kitchen napkin,set aside for 30 min.
  5. After the resting period, knead the dough again for another 5 min.Dive the dough into four parts.
  6. Grease the rolling pin and board the work surface with oil.Roll the mixture. Cut out round pieces from that.
  7. Fry the cutouts.Yours golgappa will get ready
  8. Fill the golgappa with boiled potatoes,onion,curd,tamarind sauce,crispy namkeen and lemon.Decorate it with corriander.
  9. You are ready to serve the Golgappa.
