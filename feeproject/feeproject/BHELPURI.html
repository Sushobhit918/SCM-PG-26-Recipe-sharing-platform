BHELPURI



INGRIDIENTS

  • 1 cup puffed rice
  • 1/2 cup jowar and ragi flakes
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1tbps chaat masala
  • 1 potato
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 tbps lemon juice
  • 2 tbps green chutney
  • coriander leaves

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mix the puffed rice,jowar and ragi flakes and peanuts in a bowl. Sprinkles chaat masala.
  2. Add the chopped potatoes, tomatoes and onions.
  3. Add lemon juice and green chutney and mix well.
  4. Garnish with coriander leaves.
