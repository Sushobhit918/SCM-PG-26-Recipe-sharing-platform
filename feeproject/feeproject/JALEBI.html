JALEBI



INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 lemon juice
  • 1/2 tea spoon saffron strands
  • 1 cup refine flour
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tea spoon vinegar
  • 1/2 tea spoon baking powder
  • Ghee or oil for deep frying

INSTRUCTIONS


  1. Add sugar and water in a pot, heat on medium flame.
  2. When the sugar melts, add lemon juice and keep stirring till one string consistency is achieved.
  3. Now add in saffron and mix well.Turn off the flame and aside till in use.
  4. In a mixing bowl combine refined flour,baking powder,vinegar,yogurt and mix well.Set aside for 5 to 7 min.
  5. Add 1/2 cup water and wisk till there are no lumps.
  6. Now wisk for 2 min so thwe air incorporates.
  7. Fill the prepared batter in piping bag or ketchup bottle.
  8. Heat oil for deep frying in a wide mouth pan.
  9. Form round whirls working closely friom outside to the center.
  10. Fry the jalebis till golden in colour. Remove the jalebi and immediately steep in prepared sugar syrup.
  11. Soak the jalebis for 3-4 min. Remove and arrange on a plate.
  12. Garnish with thinly chopped pistachios and almonds and serve hot.
