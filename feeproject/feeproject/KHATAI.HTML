KHATAI



INGREDIENTS

  • Maida
  • gram flour
  • ghee
  • powdered sugar
  • cardamom powder
  • Baking powder
  • baking soda
  • milk
  • pista

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degree C. Line a baking tray with foil and set aside.
  2. Take flour,gram flour,baking soda,baking powder in a bowl and mix well.
  3. Take a ghee,powdered sugar,cardamom powder in a bowl and use a whisk to mix well.
  4. Now add in the flour mix and fold well. Add milk and knead to a soft dough.
  5. Take small portion of the dough and roll it into a ball.Dip it into the chopped pista and arrange it in a tray.
  6. Bake for 15-20 min.
  7. Remove from oven and let it cool completely
  8. Store in air tight container and Serve
