KHANDVI



INGREDIENTS

  • Chickpea flour
  • Curd
  • Water
  • Powdered sugar
  • Turmeric powder
  • Iodised salt

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Put some refined oil
  2. Then one spoon of mustard seeds
  3. Put on stove at light flame along with this put some paneer and carrot. Mix it properly.
  4. Take one bowl; put chickpea,curd,sugar,water and powdwered turmeric.Mix it well.After mixing put it on another bowl after refining.
  5. Now take big wok and put it on high flame.Put mixture in that.Put half table spoon of refine oil.Stir it properly.After 10 min,stop the flame.
  6. Take full material on one slate and roll it like a spring roll.After that cut it into small pices.
  7. Put some raddish and Carrot on it and decorate it well . Yours khandvi is raedy to serve.
