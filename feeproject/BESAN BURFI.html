BESAN BURFI



INGRIDIENTS

  • Gram flour 2 cups
  • Green cardamom powder 1/2 teaspoon
  • Pistachios silvered 10-12
  • Almond silvered 10-12
  • Powdered sugar 1 cup
  • Pure ghee 1 cup + for greasing

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat the ghee in a non stick kadai;add the gram flour and saute on low heat, stirring continuously, for 10 to 15 min, or till it starts changing colour and is fragnant.
  2. Add the cardamom powder and pistachio and almond silvers, and mix. Remove from the heat and set aside to cool.
  3. Add the powdered sugar and mix well.
  4. Grease a six by eight inch aluminium straight-sided shallow tray with the ghee.Pour the gram flour mixture into the tray and spread it evenly with a greased spatula.
  5. Cool and cut into squares or diamonds and serve. Store the burfi in an airtight container.
